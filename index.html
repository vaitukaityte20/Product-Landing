Care Tips

To get the best out of our candles, we recommend:

Trim the wick to 5–7 mm before every burn.
First burn: let wax melt edge-to-edge to prevent tunneling (≈1 hour per 2.5–3 cm of diameter).
Always burn on a heat-safe, level surface away from drafts.
Never leave a burning candle unattended; keep away from kids and pets.
Limit each session to 3–4 hours; let the candle cool 2 hours before relighting.
Keep the melt pool clean—no matches, wick trimmings, or liquids.
If the wick leans, gently recenter it while the wax is soft.
Stop using with about 1 cm of wax remaining.
Store cool and out of sun. A natural beeswax “bloom” is normal—polish with a soft cloth or warm air.
