Why Medūkis Candles?

Since our beginnings as a Lithuanian apiary, Medūkis has honored tradition and craftsmanship. Each candle is hand-poured, using pure beeswax and cotton wicks—no paraffin, no dyes. Made with care, these candles bring a warm, natural glow to your home.

Eco, recyclable packaging
Antibacterial & antiviral properties

Candle Care Tips

  • Trim wick to ~5–7 mm before lighting.
  • First burn: allow wax to melt to edges to avoid tunneling.
  • Avoid drafts for a steady flame.
  • Use a snuffer to extinguish and reduce soot.
